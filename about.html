Hello! Welcome to my site!

My name is Nasim aka The Minted Girl. I love the color mint, lavender (inspired by the lavender fields) and beautiful designs! I have always been drawn to the design world, I used to practice writing fonts as a child, fascinated with the way you can shape lines and make them into different designs. I have a varied background and have lived in different countries growing up which gives me a unique and flexible perspective. I love making peoples visions into reality, designing is my passion and I would love to help you bring your vision to life.
